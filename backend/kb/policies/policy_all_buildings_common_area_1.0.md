# Common Area Usage Policy

## Resident Use
- Common areas for all residents and authorized guests
- Respect quiet hours: 10 PM - 7 AM
- Clean up after yourself
- Report damage or maintenance needs

## Reservable Spaces
- Clubhouse: $50/event, 4-hour minimum
- BBQ areas: Free, first-come first-served
- Conference room: Free for residents, by reservation

## Prohibited Activities
- Commercial activities
- Alcohol in non-designated areas
- Smoking anywhere indoors
- Loud music or parties without approval
- Damaging or removing furniture/equipment

## Party Policy
- Gatherings of 15+ people require notification
- Gatherings of 30+ require $100 deposit
- Must end by 10 PM or quiet hour start
- Violations may result in fees and loss of privileges
