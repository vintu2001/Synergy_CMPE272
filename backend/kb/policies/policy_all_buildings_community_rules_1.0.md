# Community Rules and Regulations

## General Conduct
- Respect fellow residents and staff
- Follow all posted rules and signage
- Maintain cleanliness in common areas
- Use facilities responsibly

## Prohibited Items
- Grills on balconies (fire hazard)
- Waterbeds (weight/leak risk)
- Firearms discharged on property
- Fireworks or explosives
- Hazardous materials

## Decorations
- Wreaths and seasonal decorations permitted on doors
- No exterior lights or decorations on balconies
- Holiday decorations up to 30 days
- Remove decorations promptly after holidays

## Alterations
- No structural changes without approval
- No painting without approval
- Wall anchors limited to small picture hooks
- Professional installation required for ceiling fans, fixtures

## Conduct Standards
- No threatening, violent, or aggressive behavior
- No harassment or discrimination
- Respect staff and contractors
- Violations may result in lease termination

## Enforcement
- Management reserves right to enforce policies
- Fines and penalties as specified in individual policies
- Repeated violations may result in non-renewal
- Serious violations may result in immediate lease termination
