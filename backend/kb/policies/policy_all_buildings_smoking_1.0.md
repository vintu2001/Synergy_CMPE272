# Smoking and Vaping Policy

## Designated Areas
- Smoking permitted in designated outdoor areas only
- Minimum 25 feet from building entrances
- Smoking stations marked with signage and ash receptacles

## Prohibited Areas
- Inside any building or unit
- On balconies or patios
- In parking garages
- Near air intake vents
- Within 10 feet of windows

## Violations
- First: $100 fine + written warning
- Second: $250 fine
- Third: Lease violation + possible termination

## Marijuana/Cannabis
- Subject to same rules as tobacco
- Odor complaints handled as lease violation
- Medical marijuana users must use smokeless methods indoors

## Disposal
- Use designated ash receptacles only
- No disposal in landscaping
- No littering of cigarette butts
