# Pet Policy

## Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for responsible pet ownership to ensure the comfort and safety of all residents and their pets.

## Approved Pets
**Allowed**:
- Dogs (up to 2 per unit, weight limit 50 lbs each)
- Cats (up to 2 per unit)
- Caged birds (non-exotic, up to 2)
- Fish in tanks up to 50 gallons
- Service animals (no restrictions)
- Emotional support animals (documentation required)

**Prohibited**:
- Exotic animals (reptiles, amphibians, rodents except hamsters/guinea pigs)
- Farm animals
- Aggressive breeds (Pit Bulls, Rottweilers, Dobermans, Wolf hybrids)
- Any animal over 50 lbs (except service animals)

## Registration Requirements
All pets must be registered within 10 days:
1. Complete pet registration form
2. Provide proof of vaccinations (rabies, DHPP for dogs; FVRCP for cats)
3. Provide proof of spay/neuter (dogs/cats over 6 months)
4. Submit pet photo for identification
5. Pay pet deposit: $300 per pet (refundable)
6. Pay pet rent: $25/month per pet (non-refundable)

## Pet Owner Responsibilities

### General Care
- Pets must be licensed per city requirements
- Keep vaccinations current (annual proof required)
- Maintain regular flea/tick prevention
- Groom pets regularly to minimize shedding/odor
- Never leave pets unattended for more than 10 hours

### Leash and Control
- Dogs must be leashed in all common areas
- Maximum leash length: 6 feet
- Owners must maintain control of pets at all times
- Pets not allowed in: pool areas, fitness centers, playgrounds, clubhouse
- Designated pet relief areas must be used

### Waste Management
- Clean up pet waste immediately
- Dispose of waste in designated receptacles only
- Sanitary bags available at pet stations
- Fine for not cleaning up: $50 first offense, $100 subsequent offenses

### Noise Control
- Pets must not disturb other residents
- Continuous barking/howling not permitted (more than 15 minutes)
- First complaint: verbal warning
- Second complaint: $100 fine + mandatory training evaluation
- Third complaint: pet removal required

## Prohibited Activities
- Leaving pets unattended on balconies/patios
- Bathing pets in common area sinks
- Allowing pets to damage landscaping
- Feeding stray or wild animals
- Breeding or commercial pet activities
- Pet sitting for non-residents without approval

## Damage and Liability
- Owners liable for all pet damage beyond normal wear
- Pet deposit may be applied to damages
- Additional charges billed if damages exceed deposit
- Owners liable for injuries caused by their pets
- Renters insurance must include pet liability coverage

## Inspection Rights
- Management may inspect units with 24-hour notice if pet violations suspected
- Inspection may occur immediately if safety concerns exist
- Unregistered pets discovered subject to $200 fine + immediate registration or removal

## Service and Assistance Animals
- No breed, size, or number restrictions
- No pet deposit or pet rent charged
- Documentation required: letter from licensed healthcare provider
- Must still follow noise and waste policies
- Not subject to general pet restrictions

## Temporary Pets
- Visiting pets (guests) allowed up to 7 days
- Must notify management 24 hours in advance
- Visitor pets must follow all policy requirements
- Extended visits require registration and fees

## Violations and Penalties

### First Violation
- Written warning
- 7 days to cure issue
- Mandatory review of pet policy

### Second Violation (within 6 months)
- $100 fine
- 3 days to cure issue
- Possible pet removal required

### Third Violation (within 12 months)
- $250 fine
- Immediate pet removal required (7 days)
- Pet banned from property permanently

### Serious Violations (immediate removal)
- Unprovoked biting or attacking
- Repeated aggressive behavior
- Significant property damage
- Chronic noise disturbances
- Unregistered pets after warning

## Appeals
Pet owners may appeal violations within 5 days by submitting written documentation to property manager.
