# Parking Policy

## Purpose
This policy ensures fair and efficient use of parking facilities for all residents and authorized guests.

## Parking Allocation

### Resident Parking
- **Studio/1BR**: No assigned space, can rent from additional spaces.
- **2BR**: 1 assigned space included, 2nd available for $50/month
- **3BR**: 2 assigned spaces included in rent
- **Additional spaces**: $75/month (subject to availability)
- **Reserved covered parking**: $100/month (limited availability)

## Vehicle Registration
All vehicles must be registered within 10 days:
1. Complete vehicle registration form
2. Provide proof of insurance
3. Provide vehicle make, model, color, license plate
4. Obtain and display parking permit (windshield sticker)
5. Update registration within 10 days of vehicle change

## Parking Rules

### Assigned Spaces
- Park only in your assigned space
- Do not block other vehicles or access
- Space numbers clearly marked
- Unauthorized parking in assigned spaces will be towed at owner's expense

### Common Parking Areas
- Park within marked lines
- Do not occupy multiple spaces
- No parking in fire lanes (towed immediately)
- No parking in handicapped spaces without valid placard
- No parking in loading zones (15-minute limit)

### Vehicle Requirements
**Allowed**:
- Licensed, insured, operational vehicles
- Motorcycles (in designated areas only)
- Scooters/mopeds (in designated areas only)

**Prohibited**:
- Inoperable vehicles (flat tires, expired registration, won't start)
- Commercial vehicles over 3/4 ton
- Recreational vehicles (RVs, campers, trailers)
- Boats or watercraft
- Vehicles with fluid leaks
- Vehicles missing license plates
- Vehicles with expired registration (over 30 days)

## Prohibited Activities
- Vehicle repair or maintenance (except emergency tire changes)
- Oil changes or fluid replacement
- Washing vehicles (use designated wash area only)
- Extended idling (more than 5 minutes)
- Loud music or engine revving
- Overnight storage of boats, trailers, moving trucks
- Selling or displaying vehicles for sale
- Living in vehicles

## Towing Policy

### Violations Subject to Immediate Tow (at owner's expense):
- Parking in fire lane
- Blocking emergency access
- Parking in handicapped space without placard
- Abandoned vehicles (72+ hours without movement)
- Vehicles without valid permits (after 48-hour warning notice)
- Parking in assigned space not belonging to vehicle owner
- Obstructing traffic flow or other vehicles

### Warning Violations (24-hour notice before tow):
- Expired registration
- Inoperable vehicle
- Unauthorized commercial vehicle
- Parking outside marked spaces
- Guest overstay without approval

### Towing Process
1. Vehicle photographed and documented
2. Warning sticker placed (if applicable)
3. Towing company contacted
4. Resident notified via email/text/phone
5. Vehicle towed to authorized facility
6. Resident responsible for all towing and storage fees

## Visitor Vehicles
- Service/delivery vehicles may use loading zones (15-minute limit)
- Moving trucks must be scheduled and may require temporary space reassignment

## Motorcycle and Scooter Parking
- Designated motorcycle parking areas only
- Must display valid parking permit
- Maximum 2 motorcycles per resident
- No parking on sidewalks or in vehicle spaces
- Secure with lock or cover (recommended)

## Violations and Penalties

### First Violation
- Warning notice placed on vehicle
- Email notification to resident
- 24 hours to correct

### Second Violation (within 30 days)
- $50 fine
- Final warning issued
- 24 hours to correct or vehicle will be towed

### Third Violation (within 60 days)
- Vehicle towed at owner's expense
- $100 fine
- Loss of parking privileges for 30 days

### Serious Violations (immediate tow, no warning)
- Fire lane parking
- Blocking emergency vehicles
- Handicapped space violation
- Creating safety hazard

## Snow Removal (Seasonal)
- During snow events, vehicles must be moved to alternate parking
- 24-hour notice provided when possible
- Vehicles not moved will be towed to allow plowing
- No reimbursement for towing during snow removal

## Appeals
Parking violations may be appealed within 5 days by submitting photos and written explanation to property manager.
