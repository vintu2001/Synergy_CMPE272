# Maintenance Request Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the process, priorities, and response times for maintenance requests to ensure timely resolution of issues affecting resident safety and comfort.

## Submission Methods
Residents can submit maintenance requests through:
1. **Online Portal** (preferred): Available 24/7, automatic ticket creation
2. **Mobile App**: Real-time submission with photo upload capability
3. **Email**: maintenance@apartmentmanager.com
4. **Phone**: (555) 123-4567 (M-F 8AM-6PM)
5. **In-person**: Front desk during business hours (M-F 8AM-6PM)

## Priority Levels and Response Times

### Emergency (Priority 1)
**Response Time**: Immediate (within 1 hour)
**Examples**:
- No heat in winter (below 50°F outside)
- No air conditioning in summer (above 85°F outside)
- Major water leaks or flooding
- Gas leaks or gas smell
- Electrical hazards (sparking, exposed wires)
- Broken locks compromising security
- Sewage backup
- Clogged toilet (only toilet in unit)

### Urgent (Priority 2)
**Response Time**: Within 24 hours
**Examples**:
- Partial heating/cooling failure
- Clogged sinks/basins
- Non-functioning refrigerator
- Minor water leaks
- Broken windows or doors
- Pest infestation
- Hot water issues

### Routine (Priority 3)
**Response Time**: Within 3-5 business days
**Examples**:
- Dripping faucets
- Running toilets
- Light bulb replacement (common areas)
- Cabinet/drawer repairs
- Cosmetic damage
- Appliance maintenance
- Paint touch-ups

### Planned (Priority 4)
**Response Time**: Scheduled within 7-14 days
**Examples**:
- Carpet cleaning
- Seasonal HVAC maintenance
- Preventive inspections
- Upgrade requests
- Non-essential improvements

## Required Information
All maintenance requests must include:
- Unit number
- Contact information (phone/email)
- Detailed description of issue
- Photos (if applicable)
- Preferred access time
- Pet information (if applicable)

## Entry Notification
- **Emergency**: Staff may enter immediately without notice
- **Urgent**: 24-hour notice via phone/email/door notice
- **Routine**: 48-hour notice via email and door notice
- **Planned**: 7-day advance notice

## Resident Responsibilities
- Ensure unit is accessible during scheduled maintenance
- Secure pets during maintenance visits
- Report issues promptly to prevent escalation
- Do not attempt DIY repairs on building systems
- Be present for appliance delivery/installation

## Maintenance Hours
- **Regular Hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Emergency Service**: 24/7/365
- **Weekend Service**: Saturday 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM (emergencies only)

## Charges and Fees
- **Covered by Rent**: Normal wear and tear, building system failures
- **Resident Responsibility**: Damage beyond normal wear, lost keys ($75), lockouts ($50 during business hours, $100 after hours), preventable issues (clogged drains from misuse)
- **Estimate Provided**: For repairs exceeding $200 resident responsibility

## Preventive Maintenance Schedule
- **HVAC**: Filter change quarterly, inspection bi-annually
- **Appliances**: Annual inspection
- **Plumbing**: Drain cleaning annually
- **Fire Safety**: Detector testing quarterly
- **Pest Control**: Monthly common area treatment, unit treatment on request
