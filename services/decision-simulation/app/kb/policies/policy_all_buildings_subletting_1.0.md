# Subletting and Assignment Policy

## General Rule
Subletting prohibited without written management approval.

## Application for Subletting
- Submit request 30+ days in advance
- Provide subtenant screening information
- $200 subletting administrative fee
- Maximum subletting period: 6 months

## Requirements
- Subtenant must meet income and credit requirements
- Original resident remains liable for rent and damages
- Subletting agreement must be in writing
- Management approval required before subtenant moves in

## Airbnb/Short-Term Rentals
- Strictly prohibited
- Violation results in immediate lease termination
- $500/day fine for each violation

## Lease Assignment
- Transfer of lease to new tenant requires approval
- $300 assignment fee
- New tenant must meet all application requirements
- Original resident released from obligations upon approval
