# Guest and Visitor Policy

## Purpose
Define rules for guest visits to maintain security and resident comfort.

## Guest Definition
- **Short-term**: Visits up to 7 consecutive days
- **Extended**: Visits 8-30 days (requires approval)
- **Unauthorized Occupant**: 30+ days without approval

## Guest Registration
- Register guests staying 3+ days through resident portal
- Provide: name, relationship, dates of stay, vehicle information
- Maximum 2 guests per resident at one time

## Guest Privileges
- Pool and fitness center access with resident present
- Parking in designated guest spaces only
- Building entry via resident escort or temporary access code

## Restrictions
- Guests may not create disturbances
- Resident responsible for guest behavior
- Violation of policies by guests charged to resident
- Extended stays without approval: $100/day fine
