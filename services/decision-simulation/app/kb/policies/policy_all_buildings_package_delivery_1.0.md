# Package Delivery and Mail Policy

## Purpose
Establish procedures for secure and efficient package handling for all residents.

## Package Acceptance
- Management accepts packages for residents Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM
- Package lockers available 24/7 for carriers with access codes
- Signature required items: Resident must be present or authorize management
- Perishable items: Not accepted by management (direct delivery only)

## Notification Process
Residents notified within 30 minutes via:
- Text message (if opted in)
- Email to registered address
- Mobile app push notification

## Package Retrieval
- Maximum storage: 5 business days
- Pick up during office hours or use locker access code
- Valid ID required for pickup
- Proxy pickup: Written authorization required

## Package Locker System
- Amazon Hub lockers: Available 24/7
- Access code sent via email/text
- Maximum storage: 3 days in locker
- Oversized packages: Held at front desk

## Large or Oversized Deliveries
- Furniture, appliances: Coordinate delivery time with management
- Move-in elevator reservation required
- Common area delivery not permitted (immediate unit delivery only)
- Storage in unit or off-site only

## Undeliverable or Unclaimed Packages
- After 5 days: Second notification sent
- After 10 days: Marked as abandoned
- After 15 days: Returned to sender or donated
- Resident responsible for return shipping costs if applicable

## Liability
- Management not liable for package damage, loss, or theft
- Carriers responsible for delivery issues
- Resident insurance recommended for valuable items
- Sign up for carrier tracking and delivery alerts

## Prohibited Items
Management will not accept:
- Hazardous materials
- Live animals
- Perishable food (except pre-arranged meal services)
- Items requiring refrigeration
- Weapons or ammunition
- Illegal substances

## Mailbox Access
- USPS mailboxes assigned to each unit
- Keys provided at move-in
- Lost keys: $75 replacement fee
- Forward mail within 10 days of move-out
