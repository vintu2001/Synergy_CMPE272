# Pool and Amenity Policy

## Pool Hours
- Summer (May-Sept): 7 AM - 10 PM daily
- Winter (Oct-Apr): 9 AM - 7 PM daily

## Rules
- No glass containers
- Children under 14 must have adult supervision
- No diving in shallow areas
- Swim at your own risk
- Shower before entering
- Proper swimwear required

## Fitness Center
- 24/7 access for residents with key fob
- Age 16+ only (under 18 with adult)
- Wipe down equipment after use
- Report broken equipment immediately
- Personal trainers require approval

## Violations
- First: verbal warning
- Second: 7-day amenity suspension
- Third: 30-day amenity suspension
- Serious violations: immediate permanent ban
