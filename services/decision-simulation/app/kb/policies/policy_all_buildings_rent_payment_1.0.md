# Rent Payment Policy

## Payment Due Date
Rent due on 1st of each month, no grace period.

## Payment Methods
1. **Online Portal** (preferred): No fee, instant credit
2. **ACH/Bank Transfer**: No fee, 1-2 business days
3. **Check**: Payable to property, delivered by mail or in person
4. **Money Order**: Accepted at front desk
5. **Credit/Debit Card**: 2.95% convenience fee

## Late Fees
- Day 4: $75 or 5% of rent (whichever greater)
- Day 10: Additional $25
- Day 15: Additional $50 + late notice
- Day 20: 3-Day Pay or Quit Notice

## Returned Payment
- $50 returned check/ACH fee
- Future payments required via certified funds

## Partial Payments
- Accepted at management discretion
- Applied to oldest charges first
- Does not prevent legal action

## Payment Plans
- Available for financial hardship
- Must apply before rent is 10 days late
- Requires income documentation
- Maximum 3 months to catch up
