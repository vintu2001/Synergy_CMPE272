# Noise Complaint Policy

## Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for managing noise complaints and maintaining peaceful living conditions for all residents.

## Quiet Hours
- **Weekdays**: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM
- **Weekends**: 11:00 PM - 8:00 AM
- **Holidays**: 11:00 PM - 9:00 AM

## Acceptable Noise Levels
- During quiet hours: Maximum 45 decibels (conversation level)
- During daytime: Maximum 60 decibels (normal activity level)
- Common areas: Maximum 55 decibels at all times

## Reporting Procedure
1. Residents should first attempt to resolve noise issues directly with neighbors
2. If unresolved, contact the front desk or submit a complaint through the resident portal
3. Staff will document the complaint with date, time, location, and nature of disturbance
4. For emergencies involving threats or violence, call security immediately (ext. 911)

## Response Protocol

### First Complaint
- Verbal warning issued within 24 hours
- Warning documented in resident file
- Follow-up contact within 3 business days

### Second Complaint (within 30 days)
- Written warning via email and certified mail
- $100 fine assessed to resident account
- Mandatory meeting with property manager scheduled

### Third Complaint (within 60 days)
- $250 fine assessed
- 30-day notice to cure or vacate
- Lease violation documented

### Fourth Complaint (within 90 days)
- Lease termination proceedings initiated
- All deposits subject to forfeiture
- Legal action may be pursued

## Exceptions
The following activities are permitted with 48-hour advance notice:
- Moving in/out (7:00 AM - 9:00 PM only)
- Emergency maintenance
- Building-approved events (with time restrictions)
- Religious/cultural observances (pre-approved by management)

## Special Considerations
- Residents with medical equipment that generates noise must notify management
- Service animals are exempt from pet noise restrictions
- Construction/renovation requires special permit and is limited to 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM weekdays

## Appeals Process
Residents may appeal warnings or fines within 7 days by submitting a written request to the property manager. Appeals will be reviewed within 5 business days.
