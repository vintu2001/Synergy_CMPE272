### Emergency Service (Priority 1)
- **Response Time**: 1-2 hours
- **Resolution Target**: 4 hours
- **Availability**: 24/7/365
- **Examples**: Major leaks, safety hazards, complete system failures

### Urgent Service (Priority 2)
- **Response Time**: 4-8 hours
- **Resolution Target**: 24 hours
- **Availability**: 24/7
- **Examples**: Partial failures, significant issues affecting habitability

### Routine Service (Priority 3)
- **Response Time**: Next business day
- **Resolution Target**: 3-5 business days
- **Availability**: Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM
- **Examples**: Minor repairs, preventive maintenance
