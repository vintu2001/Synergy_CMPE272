# HVAC Maintenance Service Level Agreement

## Service Provider
**Company**: Superior HVAC Solutions  
**Contact**: dispatch@superiorhvac.com  
**Emergency**: (555) 345-6790  
**Account Manager**: John Mitchell  
**Contract Period**: January 1, 2024 - December 31, 2024

## Service Scope

### Covered Systems
- Central HVAC units (12 units across property)
- Individual unit HVAC systems (145 units)
- Boiler systems (2 units)
- Ventilation systems (common areas)
- Thermostats and controls

### Preventive Maintenance Schedule

#### Quarterly (Every 3 months):
- Filter replacement (all units)
- Visual inspection of equipment
- Thermostat calibration
- Basic cleaning of accessible components

#### Bi-Annual (Spring and Fall):
- Complete system inspection
- Refrigerant level check
- Electrical connection testing
- Condensate drain cleaning
- Belt tension adjustment
- Lubrication of moving parts
- Airflow measurement

#### Annual:
- Full system cleaning
- Coil cleaning (evaporator and condenser)
- Ductwork inspection
- Control system testing
- Safety control verification
- Heat exchanger inspection
- Performance testing and documentation

## Response Time Commitments

### Emergency Service (Priority 1)
**Definition**: No heat/cooling when outdoor temperature is extreme (below 50°F or above 85°F)  
**Response Time**: 2 hours  
**Resolution Time**: 4 hours or temporary solution provided  
**Availability**: 24/7/365

### Urgent Service (Priority 2)
**Definition**: Partial system failure, significant comfort issues  
**Response Time**: 4 hours  
**Resolution Time**: 24 hours  
**Availability**: 24/7/365

### Routine Service (Priority 3)
**Definition**: Minor issues, scheduled maintenance  
**Response Time**: Next business day  
**Resolution Time**: 3 business days  
**Availability**: Monday-Friday, 7 AM - 7 PM
