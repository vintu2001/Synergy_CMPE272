# Approval Thresholds & Spending Caps

## Spending Limits by Urgency Level

### Emergency (Immediate Safety Risk)

- **Auto-approved up to:** $1,000 per incident
- **Requires maintenance supervisor approval:** $1,001 - $2,500
- **Requires property manager approval:** $2,501 - $5,000
- **Requires regional manager approval:** $5,001 - $10,000
- **Requires executive approval:** >$10,000
- **Special rules:** Life-safety issues bypass limits, approve first then document

### Urgent (24-48 Hour Response Required)

- **Auto-approved up to:** $600 per incident
- **Requires maintenance supervisor approval:** $601 - $1,500
- **Requires property manager approval:** $1,501 - $3,500
- **Requires regional manager approval:** $3,501 - $7,500
- **Requires executive approval:** >$7,500
- **Special rules:** HVAC failures in extreme weather can escalate one approval level

### Standard (72+ Hour Response Acceptable)

- **Auto-approved up to:** $300 per incident
- **Requires maintenance supervisor approval:** $301 - $800
- **Requires property manager approval:** $801 - $2,000
- **Requires regional manager approval:** $2,001 - $5,000
- **Requires executive approval:** >$5,000
- **Special rules:** Preventative maintenance bundles auto-approved to $500

### Non-Urgent (Scheduled/Planned Work)

- **Auto-approved up to:** $150 per incident
- **Requires maintenance supervisor approval:** $151 - $500
- **Requires property manager approval:** $501 - $1,500
- **Requires regional manager approval:** $1,501 - $3,000
- **Requires executive approval:** >$3,000
- **Special rules:** Cosmetic repairs require resident complaint or inspection finding

## Multi-Job & Recurring Work Limits

### Multiple Units (Same Issue)

- **2-3 units affected:** Sum total costs for approval threshold
- **4-6 units affected:** Property manager approval required regardless of per-unit cost
- **7+ units affected:** Regional manager approval required, may indicate systemic issue

### Recurring Issues (Same Unit, 90 Days)

- **Second occurrence:** Maintenance supervisor review required
- **Third occurrence:** Property manager approval + root cause analysis
- **Fourth occurrence:** Regional manager review + escalation to capital improvement

### Preventative Maintenance Programs

- **Quarterly HVAC maintenance:** Auto-approved up to $150 per unit per visit
- **Annual plumbing inspection:** Auto-approved up to $100 per unit
- **Bi-annual electrical inspection:** Auto-approved up to $125 per unit
- **Annual appliance maintenance:** Auto-approved up to $100 per unit
- **Program total per building:** Requires property manager quarterly review if exceeds $25,000

## Capital Improvement vs. Repair Threshold

### Repair Classification (Operating Budget)

- **Restores to original working condition:** Use repair budget
- **Expected useful life <5 years:** Use repair budget
- **Total cost <$2,500 per unit:** Generally repair budget

### Capital Improvement Classification (CapEx Budget)

- **Extends useful life >5 years:** Use CapEx budget
- **Significant upgrade or enhancement:** Use CapEx budget
- **Total cost >$2,500 per unit:** Generally CapEx budget
- **Multi-unit systemic upgrade:** Use CapEx budget (e.g., all HVAC units in building)
- **Approval required:** Always requires regional manager or higher approval

## After-Hours Emergency Override

### Situations Requiring Immediate Action (Override Normal Limits)

- **Active gas leak:** Immediate utility shutoff + emergency vendor dispatch
- **Structural collapse/hazard:** Immediate area closure + emergency engineering
- **Major water leak (>10 gpm):** Immediate water shutoff + emergency mitigation
- **Fire/smoke active:** Immediate evacuation + fire department + safety inspection
- **Electrical fire hazard:** Immediate circuit shutdown + emergency electrician
- **Security breach (forced entry):** Immediate locksmith + police notification

### After-Hours Approval Process

- **Step 1:** On-call maintenance supervisor notified immediately
- **Step 2:** If cost expected >$1,000, notify property manager (any hour)
- **Step 3:** If cost expected >$5,000, notify regional manager (any hour)
- **Step 4:** Document decision rationale and authorization in incident report
- **Step 5:** Follow-up review within 24 hours with photos and vendor quote

## Resident-Caused Damage Billing Thresholds

### Auto-Bill to Resident (No Approval Required)

- **Lockout service:** $125 business hours, $250 after-hours
- **Carpet cleaning (pet/spill):** Up to $200
- **Trash removal (improper disposal):** Up to $100
- **Light bulb replacement:** Up to $25
- **Filter replacement (resident responsibility per lease):** Up to $50

### Requires Property Manager Approval to Bill

- **Appliance damage (resident negligence):** $250-$1,500
- **Plumbing damage (foreign object):** $150-$800
- **Wall/door damage (beyond normal wear):** $200-$1,000
- **Flooring damage (beyond normal wear):** $300-$1,500

### Requires Legal Review Before Billing

- **Major damage >$2,000:** Document with photos, get quotes, legal letter
- **Disputed charges:** Any charge resident formally disputes in writing
- **Move-out damage >$1,500 beyond deposit:** Security deposit laws vary by state
- **Lease violation pattern:** Multiple charges may require eviction proceedings

## Annual Budget Caps by Category

### Per Building Annual Limits (Requires Budget Increase Approval if Exceeded)

- **HVAC repairs:** $25,000 per building per year
- **Plumbing repairs:** $20,000 per building per year
- **Electrical repairs:** $15,000 per building per year
- **Appliance repairs/replacement:** $18,000 per building per year
- **General maintenance:** $30,000 per building per year
- **Emergency fund reserve:** $10,000 per building per year (untouched if possible)

### Overage Approval Process

- **10-20% over budget:** Property manager provides explanation and forecast
- **20-35% over budget:** Regional manager review + corrective action plan
- **>35% over budget:** Executive review + budget reallocation or increase request
