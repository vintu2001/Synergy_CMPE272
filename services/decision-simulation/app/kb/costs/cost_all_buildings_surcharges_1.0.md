# Surcharges & Credits

## Emergency Response Surcharges

- **Active water leak (>1 gallon/hour):** +$75 emergency response fee
- **Electrical hazard (exposed wires, smoking outlet):** +$100 emergency response fee
- **Gas leak or gas smell:** +$150 emergency response fee + mandatory utility company notification
- **HVAC failure (temperature <55°F or >85°F):** +$50 emergency response fee
- **Security breach (broken lock, door damage):** +$75 emergency response fee
- **Structural hazard (ceiling damage, floor collapse):** +$150 emergency response fee
- **Active flooding (multiple units affected):** +$200 emergency response fee + water mitigation service

## After-Hours Service Surcharges

- **Weeknight after-hours (5:00 PM - 10:00 PM, Mon-Fri):** Standard rate × 1.5 multiplier
- **Late night (10:00 PM - 7:00 AM, any day):** Standard rate × 2.0 multiplier
- **Weekend (Saturday-Sunday, 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM):** Standard rate × 1.5 multiplier
- **Holiday (New Year's, July 4th, Thanksgiving, Christmas):** Standard rate × 2.0 multiplier
- **Holiday weekend (3-day weekend):** Standard rate × 1.5 multiplier on extra day

## Distance & Zone Surcharges

- **Building A (downtown location):** No surcharge, preferred zone
- **Building B (suburban location):** +$15 travel surcharge per visit
- **Building C (remote location):** +$35 travel surcharge per visit
- **Emergency callback (tech returns within 24 hours):** No additional travel fee
- **Multi-unit service (same building, same visit):** -$10 discount per additional unit after first

## Parking & Access Surcharges

- **Parking garage fee reimbursement:** Actual cost up to $20 per visit
- **Parking meter reimbursement:** Actual cost up to $10 per visit
- **Toll road reimbursement:** Actual cost with receipt
- **Building access delay (>15 minutes wait):** +$25 waiting time charge
- **Special access requirements (high-rise, restricted entry):** +$20 coordination fee

## Material Availability Surcharges

- **Expedited parts delivery (same-day):** +$50 rush fee
- **Expedited parts delivery (next-day):** +$25 rush fee
- **After-hours parts procurement:** +$40 vendor premium
- **Specialty parts (non-standard, requires special order):** +15% markup on parts cost
- **Out-of-stock parts (requires alternative vendor):** +10% markup on parts cost

## SLA Performance Credits

### Response Time SLA Credits

- **Emergency response missed (>2 hours):** -$100 credit to resident
- **Urgent response missed (>24 hours):** -$75 credit to resident
- **Standard response missed (>72 hours):** -$50 credit to resident
- **Scheduled appointment missed (no-show):** -$75 credit to resident
- **Callback not completed within SLA:** -$50 credit to resident

### Resolution Time SLA Credits

- **Emergency resolution missed (>4 hours):** -$150 credit to resident
- **Urgent resolution missed (>48 hours):** -$100 credit to resident
- **Standard resolution missed (>7 days):** -$75 credit to resident
- **Temporary repair becomes permanent (>14 days):** -$50 credit to resident

### Service Quality SLA Credits

- **Rework required within 30 days:** -$50 credit to resident + no charge for rework
- **Damage caused by technician:** -$100 credit + full repair at no charge
- **Incomplete work (missed items from scope):** -$40 credit + complete at no charge
- **Unprofessional conduct (verified complaint):** -$75 credit to resident

## Resident Service Fee Conversions

- **Late rent payment fee:** $75 if paid 1-5 days late, $150 if >5 days late
- **Lockout service (resident-caused):** $125 during business hours, $250 after-hours
- **Lost key replacement:** $25 per key + $85 rekey if security risk
- **Pet violation fine:** $200 first offense, $400 second offense, eviction proceedings third offense
- **Smoking violation fine:** $150 first offense, $300 second offense
- **Noise complaint fine (verified):** $100 first offense, $250 second offense
- **Improper disposal fine (dumpster area):** $75 per occurrence
- **Parking violation fine (unauthorized space):** $50 per occurrence

## Vendor Preference Premiums

- **Preferred vendor (contracted rate):** Standard rate, no premium
- **Approved vendor (not preferred):** +5% cost premium
- **Emergency vendor (after-hours only):** +10% cost premium
- **Non-approved vendor (exception required):** +15% cost premium + approval delay

## Multi-Service Visit Discounts

- **Second service (same visit, same unit):** -15% on second service
- **Third service (same visit, same unit):** -20% on third service
- **Preventative maintenance bundled:** -10% when combined with repair work
- **Multiple units (same building, scheduled together):** -$10 travel fee per unit after first

## Payment Method Surcharges

- **Credit card payment:** +2.5% processing fee on total invoice
- **ACH/Bank transfer:** No surcharge
- **Check payment:** No surcharge
- **Cash payment:** No surcharge
- **Payment plan requested (>$500):** +$25 administrative fee + payment schedule

## Cancellation & Rescheduling Fees

- **Cancellation <24 hours notice:** $50 cancellation fee
- **Cancellation <4 hours notice:** $100 cancellation fee
- **No-show (resident not home):** $75 no-show fee + rescheduling
- **Rescheduling requested (>48 hours notice):** No charge
- **Rescheduling requested (<48 hours notice):** $25 rescheduling fee
- **Emergency work interrupted (resident-caused):** $100 restart fee + travel charges

## Seasonal & Weather Surcharges

- **Winter weather premium (Dec-Feb, <32°F):** +10% on outdoor work
- **Extreme heat premium (Jun-Aug, >95°F):** +10% on outdoor work
- **Severe weather response (storm, flood):** +$100 emergency weather premium
- **Holiday season premium (Thanksgiving-New Year):** +15% on non-emergency work
- **Peak season HVAC (Jun-Aug heating/cooling):** +$25 high-demand surcharge

## Scope Change Surcharges

- **Additional work discovered (hidden damage):** Requires new approval if >20% cost increase
- **Scope expansion (resident requests more):** +$50 change order fee + additional costs
- **Urgent priority upgrade (standard → urgent):** +25% urgency premium
- **After-hours conversion (work extends past hours):** After-hours rate applies to overage
- **Weekend extension (work incomplete by Friday):** Weekend rates apply to completion

## Insurance & Liability Surcharges

- **Certificate of insurance requested (per vendor):** $25 administrative fee
- **Additional insured endorsement:** $50 policy modification fee
- **Liability waiver (high-risk work):** Requires legal review, +$100 processing
- **Worker's comp verification:** No charge, standard requirement
- **Bond verification (major work >$5000):** $75 administrative verification fee
